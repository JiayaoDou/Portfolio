About

Bridging the space between
industry and
interface.

I'm Jiayao Dou, an Industrial Design student at RISD with a deep focus on UI/UX and product design. I work at the intersection of physical and digital — where materials thinking meets interaction design, and human-centered research drives every decision.

I'm drawn to the questions that sit between disciplines: how should an object feel in your hand, and how should the screen that controls it feel in your mind? Getting those two answers to agree is where I spend my time.
